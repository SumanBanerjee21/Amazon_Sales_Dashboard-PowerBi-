# Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI**. The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance across stores, departments, time periods, and external conditions. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

The report allows users to explore trends, identify high-performing stores and departments, analyze seasonal patterns, and evaluate the impact of holidays on sales.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Sales Overview

This page provides a high-level summary of the entire dataset.

**Visualizations Included:**

* Total Sales KPI
* Average Weekly Sales KPI
* Average Monthly Sales KPI
* Total Stores
* Total Departments
* Yearly Sales Trend
* Sales by Store Type
* Holiday vs Non-Holiday Sales
* Weekly Sales Distribution

**Purpose:**
Understand overall business performance and identify general sales patterns.

---

### 2. Store Performance Analysis

This page focuses on analyzing sales performance across different stores.

**Visualizations Included:**

* Total Sales by Store
* Store Size vs Sales (Scatter Plot)
* Store Type Contribution
* Weekly Sales by Store (Matrix Table)

**Key Insights:**

* Store type A contributes the largest share of total revenue.
* Larger store sizes tend to generate higher sales.

---

### 3. Department Analysis

This page explores revenue contribution across departments.

**Visualizations Included:**

* Total Sales by Department
* Top 10 Departments by Sales
* Department Revenue Treemap
* Total Sales by Year and Department

**Purpose:**
Identify high-performing departments and understand departmental sales distribution.

---

### 4. Time and Seasonality Analysis

This page examines temporal sales trends and seasonal patterns.

**Visualizations Included:**

* Total Sales by Month
* Total Sales by Week
* Monthly Contribution Percentage
* Total Sales by Year
* Total Sales by Week and Holiday
* Seasonal Heatmap (Month vs Year)

**Insights:**
Seasonal patterns indicate fluctuations in sales across different months and weeks.

## Data Preparation

The following steps were performed before building the dashboard:

1. Data cleaning and removal of unnecessary columns.
2. Creation of calculated fields such as Year, Month, and Week.
3. Creation of DAX measures for KPIs and aggregated metrics.
4. Creation of bins to analyze weekly sales distribution.
